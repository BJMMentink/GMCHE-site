Saint Joseph’s Atelier

art lessons for the Catholic homeschool student Spring 2026 Edition with new class openings
Saint Joseph’s Atelier is a place of learning and working for Catholic Children of God who wish to explore and look deeply, to discover and work mindfully - praising and delighting in God our Father through creativity.
We engage with elements of art and principles of design and seek classic artists as models and teachers who have embraced beauty truth and goodness through a variety of media.
We have fun discovering gifts that God has planted in each one of us, knowing that as we learn and express ourselves through visual arts, we are praising and delighting in our Father as He delights in us.
*Atelier: an artists studio or workshop - French
