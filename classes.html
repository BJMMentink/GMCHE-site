The Nitty Gritty

Class sessions, dates, pricing, and what each student can expect.

Pricing

Each student: $20 per art session.
There are 8 sessions x $20.00 = $160.00 per semester.
Each session is 3 hours long. That's $6.67/hour.

What's Included

This price includes my planning and instruction, plus all supplies used for formal art projects: tempera paints, watercolor paint, paint brushes, quality drawing paper, watercolor papers, ink, glue, spray fixative, chalk pastels, oil pastels, fabric for ink block printing, rubber blocks for linoleum block printing, clear acetate sheets, and more.

Prices for art supplies have risen considerably since I taught art for $15.00 per session. The class price has increased to cover the increased cost of supplies.

Students Bring

A good pair of scissors
and socks or slippers to wear in the atelier.

I ask that each new student pays for 3 sessions at the outset.

Class Structure

When are the classes?
Wednesdays and Fridays, with possible Thursday afternoon sessions.

We will meet on the first and third Fridays of the month.
