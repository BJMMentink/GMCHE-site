Pricing

This price includes: My planning and instruction & all supplies used for formal art projects:
(tempera paints, watercolor paint, paint brushes, quality drawing paper, watercolor papers, ink, glue, spray fixative, chalk pastels, oil pastels, fabric for ink block printing - rubber blocks for linoleum block, clear acetate sheets etc.)
